Dolo

Take 1 tablet every 6–8 hours after meals to relieve pain and reduce fever. Do not exceed 3 doses a day.

Paracetamol

Take 1 tablet every 4–6 hours for fever or pain. Maximum 4 grams per day (usually not more than 8 tablets).

Aspirin

Take 1 tablet daily after food for heart protection or 1 tablet every 4–6 hours for pain relief. Follow doctor’s dosage for long-term use.

Ibuprofen

Take 1 tablet every 6–8 hours with food. Helps relieve pain, inflammation, and fever. Avoid on an empty stomach.

Cold Relief Tablets

Take 1 tablet every 8–12 hours. Provides relief from cold symptoms like sneezing, nasal congestion, and body aches.

Vitamin C Tablets

Take 1 tablet daily, preferably in the morning. Supports immunity and helps reduce oxidative stress.

Nasal Hydration Serum

Apply 2–3 drops into each nostril as needed for dryness. Can be used 2–3 times a day or as prescribed.

Vitamin E Serum

Apply 1–2 drops on the face at night. Massage gently. Avoid contact with eyes.

Caffeine Serum

Apply under the eyes in the morning to reduce puffiness and dark circles. Use sparingly.

Hyaluronic Acid Serum

Apply on clean, damp skin morning and night. Follow with moisturizer for best hydration.

Collagen Boosting Serum

Apply to face and neck twice daily to support skin firmness and elasticity.

Anti-aging Serum

Use every night on clean skin. Massage gently onto face and neck. Pair with sunscreen in the morning.
